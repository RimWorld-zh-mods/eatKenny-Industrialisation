Adds some heavy industries to the game.

Original Author: eatKenny  
Current Maintainer: duduluu  

[h1][b]Compatible with existing save and other mods, enjoy it.[/b][/h1]

[h1][b]How to build a deep mine:[/b][/h1]
 1. Buy a 'skydriller callmaker'
 2. Install and active it
 3. Wait for the plasma beam to make a 'mine hole'
 4. Build a 'deep mine' on the mine hole

[h1][b]New stuff:[/b][/h1]
 - concrete  
 - high-grade steel  
 - copper  
 - aluminium

[h1][b]New bench:[/b][/h1]
 - concrete mixer
 - electric arc furnace
 - deep mine

[h1][b]New power building:[/b][/h1]
 - industrial cable
 - industrial battery
 - nuclear power plant

[h1][b]New trader ship:[/b][/h1]
 - deep space mining ship
